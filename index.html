About Netal

Psychologist & Professor
Based in Bangalore

Hi, I'm Netal Marothiya — a licensed psychologist and college professor in Bangalore. I combine academic depth with real-world clinical practice to offer therapy that is both evidence-based and deeply compassionate.

I specialize in helping individuals navigate anxiety, depression, academic stress, relationship challenges, and workplace burnout. As a professor, I stay current with the latest psychological research, which I translate into practical tools you can use every day.

I offer sessions online (Google Meet / Zoom) and in-person in Bangalore. My approach is warm, non-judgmental, and goal-oriented — you'll leave each session with clarity and actionable steps.

  • Licensed psychologist with 7+ years of clinical practice
  • Active psychology professor at a Bangalore college
  • Trained in Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)
  • Specialized in anxiety, depression & stress management
